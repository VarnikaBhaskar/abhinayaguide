GREEVA BHEDA


Greeva bheda refers to the neck movements used in Bharathanatyam. According to the Abhinaya Darpana, there are 4 types of neck movements:

Sundareescha Tirascheena tathaiva Parivartita
Prakampitacha bhavagney gneya greeva chaturvidha


Sundari

Gently moving the neck from side to side.

Used to express deep happiness, love or shyness.


Tirascheena

Moving the neck from side to side in a zig-zag manner.

Used to show the movement of a snake.


Parivartita

Swift and sudden movement of the neck to one side.

Used to express deep disgust or dislike.


Prakampita

Moving the neck front and back.

Used to show movement of a bird.

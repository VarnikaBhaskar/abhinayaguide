BEGINNER'S GUIDE TO ABHINAYA

-by Varnika Bhaskar


The word Abhinaya is derived from the Sanskrit words abhi, meaning towards, and ni, meaning to lead. Thus, Abhinaya is the art of leading the audience towards experiencing a particular emotion.



There are 4 aspects of Abhinaya:
  • Aangika- Expression through body movements and gestures
  • Vaachika- Expression through words
  • Aahaarya- Expression through external adornments
  • Saathvika- Expression through emotions

These 4 features of Abhinaya, embodied by Lord Shiva, are elaborated through the following shloka:

Angikam bhuvanam yasya
Vachikam sarva vaangmayam
Aharyam chandra taradi
Tam namah Satvikam Shivam

About Us


Welcome to my Abhinaya Guide. I’ve created this platform to help you learn the basics of Abhinaya.
Abhinaya is the art of leading the audience towards an aesthetic experience through subtle movements, gestures, facial expressions, and emotions.
My mission is to make learning the basics of Abhinaya easier and more accessible. So if you’re a beginner who struggles with expressing yourself to an audience, this is just the place for you!

Here you will find guidance on:

  • The fundamentals of Abhinaya and its roles in Bharatanatyam
  • Shiroh Bheda- the 9 types of head movements
  • Greeva Bheda- the 4 types of neck movements
  • Drishti Bheda- the 8 types of eye movements
  • Bhru Bheda- the 6 types of eyebrow movements
  • Navarasa- the 9 fundamental human emotions
Through this platform, I hope to support budding dancers in strengthening their foundation and finding a deeper appreciation for this art form.
Thank you for being a part of this journey!
